PANCAKES

HOME

DESCRIPTION

High carb meal to give you just the energy you need to start your day

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk or Water
  • Butter

STEPS

  1. Mix the flour, sugar and milk in a bowl to make a batter
  2. Pour the batter to a pan and wait for it to cook
  3. Flip over the pancake once cooked to desired colour
  4. Serve the pancake with a small piece of butter

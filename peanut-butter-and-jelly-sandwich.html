Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

HOME

DESCRIPTION

A simple to make on the go meal with as little as 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanutbutter
  • Jelly or Jam

STEPS

  1. Spread the peanut on one slice of the bread
  2. Spread the jelly or jam on the other slice of bread
  3. Place one slice on top of the other
